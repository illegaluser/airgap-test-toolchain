💡 Tip for recording hover menus (dropdowns / GNB) — codegen only records clicks and ignores mouse hover. Hover over the parent menu without clicking (clicking would navigate away and produce a scenario different from your intent). Once the submenu expands, click only the item you want. Multi-level menus (2-3 levels) work the same way — only the leaf click is recorded once, and during replay the visibility healer automatically cascade-hovers the parent menus to expand them.
